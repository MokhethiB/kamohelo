BEGINNER

BIO| IGCSE

IGCSE is an internationally recognised qualification for secondary school students, typically taken by students aged 14 to 16. IGCSE provides a broad and balanced Education while also letting students develop essential skills and knowledge.

INTERMEDIATE

BIO| O LEVEL

O Level is the final certification for secondary school, to be taken at fifth form or year 11 at approximately age 17 (or age group 14-16).

ADVANCED

BIO| AS LEVEL

Advanced Subsidiary level (AS level) is the first part of A Level.
